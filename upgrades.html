Character Upgrades

This page covers all major upgrade systems in Mournstead, including weapon upgrades, rune systems, healing improvements, and Umbral lamp enhancements.

Weapon Upgrading

Weapons can be upgraded using Deralium materials at the blacksmith. Higher tiers increase the base weapon damage and also slightly improve scaling.

Upgrade Materials

  • Deralium Fragment – upgrades up to +2
  • Deralium Nugget – upgrades from +2 to +5
  • Deralium Shard – upgrades from +5 to +9
  • Deralium Chunk – upgrade to +10

Notes

  • Higher-tier materials are limited and often tied to exploration.
  • Some materials can be purchased at various vendors later after they expand their stock. This is tied to progression in the game.

Rune System

Runes provide additional bonuses to weapons and can be socketed into rune slots.

Rune Slots

  • Weapons unlock rune slots as they are upgraded. The first rune slot unlocks at upgrade +1, the second one at +4 and the third and the last one at +9.
  • Different weapons may have different slot configurations.

Rune Tablets

Rune Tablets are required to unlock advanced rune functionality at the blacksmith. To unlock rune slots, the player needs to deliver rune tablets to the blacksmith. For unlocking the possibility to use the first slot, deliver Cracked Rune Tablet, for the second slot to be available, deliver Chipped Rune Tablet and for the third one, deliver Rune Tablet.

Tablet types

  • Cracked Rune Tablet – 1st rune slot
  • Chipped Rune Tablet – 2nd rune slot
  • Rune Tablet – 3rd rune slot

Usage

  • Runes can modify damage, scaling, or add effects
  • There are four rune socket types: strength, agility, radiance and inferno

Healing Upgrades

The main reusable healing item in Lords of the Fallen (2023) is Sanquinarix. Its healing ability can be improved by bringing Saintly Quintessence items to Pieta in Skyrest Bridge.

Upgrade Effects

  • Increased number of uses

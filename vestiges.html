Vestiges

This page lists all vestiges in the game, grouped by location.

Skyrest Bridge

  • Vestige of Ethryg

    Located in the center of Skyrest bridge hub area.

    Notes: Available always.

Defiled Sepulchre

  • Vestige of Ranik

    Located just outside the Sepulchre exit.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

Abandoned Redcopse

  • Vestige of Hannelore

    Located near the Redcopse village entrance.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Marco the Axe

    Located near the Redcopse windmill.

    Notes: Available up to minor vestige decay.

Pilgrim's Perch

  • Vestige of the Chabui

    Located in the Sanctuary of Baptism, just before the transition to the night side of the Perch.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Blind Agatha

    Found in the Ballroom along the upper walkways of Pilgrim's Perch after navigating several ladders and narrow cliffside passages.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

Forsaken Fen

  • Vestige of Olleren

    Located near the Fen lookout.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Valade

    Found near the entrance of the swamp area after descending from Pilgrim's Perch.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Pale Butcher

    Located at the entrance to the Shuja Hamlet.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

Fitzroy's Gorge

  • Vestige of Betrayed Eliard

    Located in the Shrine of Adyr.

    Notes: Available always.

Lower Calrath

  • Vestige of Sebastian

    Located in the burning ruined depot at the start of the area.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Lydia the Numb Witch

    Located at the top floor of the alehouse dividing two main parts of the area.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

Upper Calrath

  • Vestige of Doln

    Found in the mining district at the start of the area.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of the Forgotten Guardian

    Located at the top of the elevator from Sunless Skein.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

Sunless Skein

  • Vestige of Hooded Antuli

    Located at the start of the mines.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Catrin

    Located next to the previously flooded area near the shortcut elevator to Revelation Depths.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

Revelation Depths

  • Vestige of Lost Berescu

    Positioned near the bottom of the shortcut elevator from Sunless Skein.

    Notes: Available up to minor vestige decay.

Fief of the Chill Curse

  • Vestige of Svornil

    Located among the ruined structures at the entrance of the frozen settlement.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Loash

    Located just before the Crow's Nest.

    Notes: Available up to minor vestige decay.

Path of Devotion

  • Vestige of Dieter

    Located at the Path of Devotion Memorial.

    Notes: Available up to minor vestige decay.

Manse of the Hallowed Brothers

  • Vestige of Ferrers the Charred

    Found on the Manse Supply Pathway at the start of the arrea.

    Notes: Only available without vestige decay.

  • Vestige of Brother Jeremiah

    Found inside the central leprosarium.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

Abbey of the Hallowed Sisters

  • Vestige of Rosamund

    Located within the sacred courtyard of the Abbey complex.

    Notes: Available up to minor vestige decay.

The Empyrean

  • Vestige of Iorelo the Cursed Knight

    Located near the area's entrance.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

Bramis Castle

  • Vestige of the Bloody Pilgrim

    Located at the courtyard in front of the entrance to the main castle building.

    Notes: Available up to extensive vestige decay.

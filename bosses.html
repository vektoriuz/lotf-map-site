Bosses

This page lists all bosses in the game, divided into main bosses and minor bosses.

Main Bosses

Early Game

  • Pieta, She of Blessed Renewal

    Location: Skyrest Bridge

    Notes: First major boss encounter and introduction to several core combat mechanics.

  • The Congregator of Flesh

    Location: Forsaken Fen

    Notes: N/A

  • The Hushed Saint

    Location: Forsaken Fen

    Notes: Mounted boss that requires careful timing and positioning.

Mid Game

  • Spurned Progeny

    Location: Upper Calrath

    Notes: Massive fire-based creature occupying the burning streets of Calrath.

  • Hollow Crow

    Location: Fief of the Chill Curse

    Notes: Large creature surrounded by frozen villagers.

  • Harrower Dervla, the Pledged Knight / The Unbroken Promise

    Location: Revelation Depths

    Notes: Only mandatory for the Umbral ending.

Late Game

  • Tancred, Master of Castigations / Reinhold the Immured

    Location: Tower of Penance

    Notes: Dual-phase encounter involving Tancred and Reinhold.

  • Judge Cleric, the Radiant Sentinel

    Location: The Empyrean

    Notes: Radiant-aligned boss representing the authority of the church.

  • Lightreaper

    Location: Abandoned Redcopse, Gorge, Fief, Upper Calrath

    Notes: Recurring boss that appears several times throughout the game.

  • The Sundered Monarch

    Location: Bramis Castle

    Notes: Late-game boss guarding the castle interior.

  • Adyr, the Bereft Exile

    Location: Bramis Castle

    Notes: Final boss for the Radiance ending.

  • Elianne the Starved

    Location: Revelation Depths

    Notes: Final boss for the Umbral ending.

Minor Bosses

Early Game

  • Holy Bulwark Otto

    Location: Abandoned Redcopse

    Notes: Tutorial boss.

  • Scourged Sister Delyth

    Location: Pilgrim's Perch

    Notes: Early Radiant enemy encountered along the pilgrimage path.

  • Gentle Gaverus, Mistress of Hounds

    Location: Pilgrim's Perch

    Notes: Early Radiant enemy encountered along the pilgrimage path.

  • Mendacious Visage

    Location: Forsaken Fen

    Notes: Later found as an enemy in various locations.

  • Crimson Rector Percival

    Location: Fitzroy's Gorge

    Notes: N/A.

  • Ruiner

    Location: Fitzroy's Gorge

    Notes: Powerful Rhogar warrior encountered multiple times.

Mid Game

  • Infernal Enchantress

    Location: Lower Calrath

    Notes: N/A.

  • Skinstealer

    Location: Sunless Skein

    Notes: Aggressive enemy guarding deeper sections of the mines.

  • Bringers

    Location: Sunless Skein

    Notes: Optional bossfight lockated in the Cistern. It consists of three entities, Bringer of Stillness, Bringer of Silence and Bringer of Nullity.

  • Kinrangr Guardian Folard

    Location: Fief of the Chill Curse

    Notes: There is a Mendacious Visage present in the umbral version of the boss arena.

  • Griefbound Rowena

    Location: Fief of the Chill Curse

    Notes: N/A.

Late Game

  • The Sacred Resonance of Tenacity

    Location: Pilgrim's Perch

    Notes: Bell helm.

  • Paladin's Burden

    Location: Path of Devotion

    Notes: N/A.

  • Abiding Defenders

    Location: Manse of the Hallowed Brothers

    Notes: Dual enemy encounter testing defensive awareness.

  • Blessed Carrion Knight Sanisho

    Location: Tower of Penance

    Notes: Brace for poison.

  • Abbess Ursula

    Location: Abbey of the Hallowed Sisters

    Notes: A lot of annoying teleporting.

  • Rapturous Huntress of the Dusk

    Location: Abbey of the Hallowed Sisters

    Notes: Optional, depending on the chosen ending.

  • General Engstrom

    Location: Fitzroy's Gorge

    Notes: Secret optional boss.

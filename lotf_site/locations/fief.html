Fief of the Chill Curse

Overview

Fief of the Chill Curse is a frozen region far from the central lands of Mournstead. Blizzards, icy ruins, and cursed inhabitants define the harsh landscape.

Connections

  • Abandoned Redcopse

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Svornil
  • Vestige of Loash

Notes

The harsh environment and limited visibility make combat more dangerous. Several paths through the region loop back toward earlier areas once unlocked.

Bosses

  • Kinrangr Guardian Folard
  • Griefbound Rowena
  • Hollow Crow
  • Lightreaper (third encounter)

Enemies

  • Frozen villagers
  • Ice-bound warriors
  • Umbral creatures lurking in the snow

Important Items

  • Frost-themed equipment
  • Rare upgrade materials

NPCs

  • Occasional NPC questline appearances

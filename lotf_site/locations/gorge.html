Fitzroy's Gorge

Overview

Fitzroy's Gorge is a rugged canyon region carved through the mountains of Mournstead. Broken bridges and narrow pathways create a dangerous traversal area where enemies often attack from elevated positions.

Connections

  • Forsaken Fen
  • Skyrest Bridge
  • Lower Calrath

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Betrayed Eliard

Notes

Enemy ambushes from above are common. Exploration in Umbral often reveals alternate routes through collapsed sections of the canyon.

Bosses

  • Crimson Rector Percival
  • Ruiner
  • Lightreaper (second encounter)
  • General Engstrom

Enemies

  • Rhogar soldiers
  • Fire-wielding cultists
  • Ambushing cliffside attackers

Important Items

  • Various fire-related upgrade materials
  • Hidden Umbral loot along cliff edges

NPCs

  • Occasional story NPC encounters

Forsaken Fen

Overview

Forsaken Fen is a swamp-like region filled with corruption, poison pools, and twisted remnants of the Church of Orian Radiance. Navigation here is slower and visibility is reduced due to dense vegetation and murky terrain.

Connections

  • Pilgrim's Perch
  • Fitzroy's Gorge

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Olleren
  • Vestige of Valade
  • Vestige of Pale Butcher

Notes

The environment encourages careful movement due to poison hazards and hidden enemies. Many paths loop back toward earlier areas once key shortcuts are unlocked.

Bosses

  • The Congregator of Flesh
  • Mendacious Visage
  • The Hushed Saint

Enemies

  • Fen Guardians
  • Swamp Mutants
  • Radiant Fanatics
  • Poisonous creatures of the marsh

Important Items

  • Radiant upgrade materials
  • Various Umbral parasites hidden throughout the swamp

NPCs

  • Occasional NPC encounters tied to story progression

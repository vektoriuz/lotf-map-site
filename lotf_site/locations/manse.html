Manse of the Hallowed Brothers

Overview

The Manse of the Hallowed Brothers is a fortified religious complex dedicated to the teachings of Orian Radiance. Its halls are heavily guarded by devout warriors and clergy loyal to the church.

The Manse contains multiple interconnected rooms and hidden passages, many of which are revealed through Umbral exploration.

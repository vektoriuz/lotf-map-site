Revelation Depths

Overview

Revelation Depths lies far beneath the surface of Mournstead, where the influence of Umbral grows strongest. Ancient ruins and grotesque creatures dominate the environment.

Connections

Notes

The deeper sections of this area contain powerful enemies and dangerous environmental hazards. Exploration in Umbral reveals numerous hidden passages.

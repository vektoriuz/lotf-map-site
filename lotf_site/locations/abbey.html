Abbey of the Hallowed Sisters

Overview

The Abbey of the Hallowed Sisters serves as the spiritual center of the Radiant order. The abbey is filled with devoted followers and powerful priestesses guarding sacred relics.

Notes

The abbey serves as the gateway to the most sacred areas of the Radiant order and leads directly to the final spiritual stronghold.

Bramis Castle

Overview

Bramis Castle stands as one of the final strongholds of Rhogar influence in Mournstead. The fortress is heavily defended and filled with powerful enemies guarding the path toward the end of the journey.

Connections

  • Upper Calrath

Vestiges

  • Vestige of the Bloody Pilgrim

Notes

The castle interior is heavily guarded and filled with dangerous enemy formations. Careful exploration reveals hidden passages through the fortress walls.

Bosses

  • The Sundered Monarch
  • Adyr, the Bereft Exile

Enemies

  • Elite Rhogar knights
  • Infernal spellcasters
  • Heavy armored guardians

Important Items

  • Late-game equipment
  • Powerful upgrade materials

NPCs

  • Final stages of several NPC questlines

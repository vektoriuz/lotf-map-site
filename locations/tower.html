Tower of Penance

Overview

Tower of Penance is a grim vertical structure used to imprison and punish those accused of heresy. The tower’s interior is filled with tortured prisoners and fanatical guards enforcing the will of the church.

Connections

  • Manse of the Hallowed Brothers

Vestiges

  • Vestige located near the base of the tower

Notes

Vertical exploration dominates this area, with ladders and staircases leading through multiple levels of the tower.

Bosses

  • Blessed Carrion Knight Sanisho
  • Tancred, Master of Castigations / Reinhold the Immured

Enemies

  • Penitent prisoners
  • Radiant wardens
  • Armored church guards

Important Items

  • Rare armor pieces
  • Upgrade materials hidden in upper tower levels

NPCs

  • Occasional questline encounters

Path of Devotion

Overview

Path of Devotion is a narrow pilgrimage route leading toward the religious strongholds of Mournstead. The path winds through ruined structures and guarded passages controlled by the followers of Orian Radiance.

Connections

  • Pilgrim's Perch
  • Skyrest Bridge

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Dieter

Notes

The area functions mainly as a transition between the mountainous pilgrimage routes and the larger religious complexes deeper within Mournstead.

Bosses

Enemies

  • Armed pilgrims
  • Defenders of the sacred path

Important Items

  • Upgrade materials hidden along the path

NPCs

The Empyrean

Overview

The Empyrean represents the highest sanctum of the Radiant order. Bathed in blinding light, this sacred complex stands as the culmination of the church’s power within Mournstead.

Connections

  • Abbey of the Hallowed Sisters

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Iorelo the Cursed Knight

Notes

The Empyrean marks the climax of the Radiant storyline and serves as one of the final major areas before the end of the game.

Bosses

  • Judge Cleric, the Radiant Sentinel

Enemies

  • Radiant elite guardians
  • Holy defenders of the sanctum

Important Items

  • Endgame equipment
  • Powerful Radiance relics

NPCs

  • Final stages of several NPC questlines

Defiled Sepulchre

Overview

Defiled Sepulchre is a corrupted burial complex buried beneath the surface of Mournstead. The area contains dense enemy encounters and strong Umbral presence.

Connections

  • Abandoned redcopse

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Ranik

Notes

The narrow corridors make combat dangerous, especially when surrounded. Exploring in Umbral often reveals additional shortcuts and hidden loot.

Bosses

  • Minor elite enemies guarding deeper passages

Enemies

  • Undead guardians
  • Umbral parasites
  • Burial sentinels

Important Items

  • Weapon upgrade materials
  • Hidden loot accessible via Umbral traversal

NPCs

  • No major NPC hub presence

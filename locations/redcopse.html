Abandoned Redcopse

Overview

Abandoned Redcopse serves as one of the earliest forest regions of the game. The area introduces players to the dual-world mechanic between Axiom and Umbral and contains several branching paths leading deeper into Mournstead.

Connections

  • Defiled Sepulchre
  • Skyrest Bridge
  • Fief of the Chill Curse

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Hannelore
  • Vestige of Marco the Axe

Notes

This area functions as an early crossroads that teaches exploration mechanics and hidden Umbral paths. Several shortcuts connect back toward Skyrest Bridge later in the game.

Bosses

  • Holy Bulwark Otto
  • Lightreaper (initial encounter)

Enemies

  • Corrupted pilgrims
  • Umbral creatures emerging from the veil
  • Crossbow sentinels

Important Items

  • Umbral Lamp tutorial elements
  • Basic upgrade materials

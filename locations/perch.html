Pilgrim's Perch

Overview

Pilgrim's Perch is a vertical cliffside region filled with precarious walkways, collapsed structures, and abandoned pilgrimage routes.

Connections

  • Skyrest Bridge
  • Forsaken Fen
  • Path of Devotion
  • Manse of the Hallowed Brothers

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Chabui
  • Vestige of Blind Agatha

Notes

Vertical exploration is the main challenge in this area. Players must navigate ladders, bridges, and narrow ledges while avoiding ranged attacks.

Bosses

  • Scourged Sister Delyth
  • Gentle Gaverus, Mistress of Hounds
  • The Sacred Resonance of Tenacity

Enemies

  • Pilgrim zealots
  • Radiant defenders
  • Ranged crossbow enemies positioned along cliffs

Important Items

  • Radiance-themed equipment
  • Various consumables and upgrade materials

NPCs

  • Occasional questline interactions

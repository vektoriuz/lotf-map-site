Upper Calrath

Overview

Upper Calrath represents the higher districts of the ruined city. Collapsed buildings and burning structures create a maze-like environment above the devastated lower streets.

Connections

  • Lower Calrath
  • Skyrest Bridge
  • Sunless Skein
  • Bramis Castle

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Doln
  • Vestige of the Forgotten Guardian

Notes

Vertical traversal and rooftop pathways are common in this area. Players must navigate burning structures while dealing with strong Rhogar forces.

Bosses

  • Spurned Progeny
  • Lightreaper (final encounter)

Enemies

  • Elite Rhogar warriors
  • Infernal spellcasters
  • Armored demon guards

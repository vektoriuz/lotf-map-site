Sunless Skein

Overview

Sunless Skein is a sprawling underground mining complex beneath Mournstead. Twisting tunnels, abandoned machinery, and Umbral corruption dominate the environment.

Connections

  • Upper Calrath
  • Lower Calrath
  • Revelation Depths

Vestiges

  • Vestige of Hooded Antuli
  • Vestige of Catrin

Notes

The labyrinth-like tunnels make navigation challenging. Several hidden paths only appear when traveling through Umbral.

Bosses

  • Skinstealer
  • Bringers

Enemies

  • Corrupted miners
  • Umbral parasites
  • Rhogar intruders occupying the tunnels

Important Items

  • Upgrade materials hidden in mining chambers
  • Various Umbral resources

NPCs

  • Occasional NPC progression encounters
